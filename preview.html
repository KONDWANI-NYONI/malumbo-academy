About Our Academy

Our Mission & Vision

Malumbo Christian Academy is dedicated to providing a Christ-centered education that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential academically, spiritually, and socially.

Founded in 2005, we have been serving the community with excellence in education for over 18 years. Our dedicated staff and faculty work together to create a nurturing environment where students can thrive.

We believe in developing the whole child - mind, body, and spirit - through a balanced curriculum that integrates faith, learning, and service.
